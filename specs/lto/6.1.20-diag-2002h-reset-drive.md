# 6.1.20 Diag - 2002h: Reset Drive

This diagnostic aborts all current drive operations and restarts the functional microcode. This reset is equivalent to a power on reset. See 5.2.36--SEND DIAGNOSTIC - 1Dh and 5.2.23--RECEIVE DIAGNOSTIC RESULTS - 1Ch for additional information on the commands.

## 6.1.20.1 SendDiag Parm Data - Reset Drive
